Know Thyself: Transferable Visuomotor Control
Through Robot-Awareness
  • Anonymous
We address the problem of transferring a policy trained on one robot to another robot. Our method, Robot-Aware Control (RAC), composes a robot-invariant world dynamics model with a robot-specific analytical dynamics model and optimizes a robot-aware planning cost to enable zero-shot transfer to unseen robots.
